The Conversation Radar Agent surfaces high-signal Reddit discussions about your brand, competitors, and tracked prompts so you can influence how AI models cite and describe your category.

---

## What it looks for

- Reddit threads mentioning your brand or competitors
- Discussions tied to your tracked prompts and the problems you solve
- High-engagement posts with authority signals

---

## How it filters

Each discovered conversation gets a relevance score. Only threads scoring **70 or above** are surfaced, ranked by impact (high, medium, low). The agent tracks upvotes, comments, and engagement metrics.

---

## How it helps AI visibility

Reddit threads are frequently crawled by AI models and cited in responses. By identifying where your category is discussed, you can:

- Contribute accurate context and share resources in high-visibility threads
- Build mentions in locations that AI models reference
- Shape the narratives models reuse when answering questions about your category

Over time, this influences:

- Which sources models cite in answers
- How models describe your brand and competitors
- Your Share of Voice for key prompts
