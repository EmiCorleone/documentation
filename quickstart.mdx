<Tip>
You will be measuring AI visibility in minutes. Follow the steps below — each one aligns with how AI models crawl, rank, and cite your brand.
</Tip>

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion icon="link" title="Step 1 — Connect your domain">
    Add your brand domain. Morphiq Labs crawls key pages and mirrors how AI models see your site.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion icon="building" title="Step 2 — Set up your brand profile">
    Enter your company name, website, description, industry, competitors, and ideal customer profile. Morphiq Labs uses this to generate relevant tracked prompts automatically.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion icon="globe" title="Step 3 — Select countries and languages">
    Choose the geographies you want to track. Morphiq Labs runs separate analyses per country so you see region-specific visibility.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion icon="bullseye" title="Step 4 — Review and customize prompts">
    Morphiq Labs auto-generates prompts based on your profile. Add, remove, or edit prompts to match how your buyers actually search. Cover brand, category, competitor, and problem-based queries.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion icon="chart-line" title="Step 5 — Review your issues">
    After analysis, Morphiq Labs surfaces prioritized issues across your pages — missing schema, incomplete metadata, absent FAQs, thin content. Each issue shows its impact on your score.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion icon="wrench" title="Step 6 — Fix with one click">
    Click the Fix button on any issue. Morphiq Labs deploys the right agent, generates the fix, and creates a GitHub PR. Review, merge, and your score improves on the next analysis. For no-code platforms, click Generate Code to get copy-ready output.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

<Tip>
Morphiq Labs auto-generates prompts during onboarding based on your brand profile. Review them and add prompts spanning brand, category, competitor, and problem-based queries to maximize visibility insights.
</Tip>

## Next actions

- Verify structured data (schema, llms.txt) is present after agents run.
- Watch the first visibility reads for top prompts and confirm citation quality.
- Check your AI Referred Traffic dashboard once the tracking script is installed.
