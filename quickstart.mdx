<Callout title="You’ll be measuring AI visibility in ~10 minutes" icon="rocket">
Follow the four-step flow below. Each step aligns with how AI models crawl, rank, and cite your brand.
</Callout>

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion icon="link" title="Step 1 — Connect your domain">
    Add your brand domain. Mudra crawls key pages and mirrors how AI models crawl and understand your site.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion icon="chart-line" title="Step 2 — Measure AI visibility">
    Mudra runs brand and category prompts across top AI models. It tracks visibility, rank, sentiment, citations, and competitors.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion icon="bullseye" title="Step 3 — Add tracked prompts">
    Add personalized tracked prompts you think your ideal customer may ask. Analyze how your brand ranks against them.
</Accordion>
  <Accordion icon="robot" title="Step 4 — Deploy agents">
    File/infra agents keep structured data clean. Content agents orchestrate AI-optimized, citation-driven pages. Outreach agents secure new citations on AI-cited sources.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

<Tip>
Add 10–15 tracked prompts spanning brand, category, competitor, and “what should I use for X?” queries to accelerate visibility insights.
</Tip>

## Next actions

- Verify structured data (schema, llms.txt) is present after agents run.
- Watch the first visibility reads for top prompts and confirm citation quality.
- Schedule weekly prompt runs to track movement as agents adapt your site.
