AI Referred Traffic helps you understand how much of your traffic is influenced by AI. Morphiq Labs gives you a way to track traffic that originates from AI answers and assistants.

## What counts as AI Referred Traffic?

AI Referred Traffic is an estimate of visits that start or are heavily influenced by an interaction with an AI model, and eventually arrive on your site.

### Setup

Add our lightweight tracking script to your site’s `<head>`. Until the script is installed, the dashboard shows “Not connected.”

### What models are tracked?

The tracking script detects referrals from four AI models:

- **ChatGPT**
- **Claude**
- **Perplexity**
- **Gemini**

Your dashboard displays total AI referral visits plus a per-model breakdown, with period-over-period comparison.

It is not a perfect, low-level “referrer” field. Instead, it’s a **directional signal** to answer:

> “As we improve AI visibility and structure, are we also seeing more visits that look like they are coming from AI-driven discovery?”

---

## Why it matters

AI visibility without traffic is just a vanity metric. AI Referred Traffic helps you:

- Connect visibility and structure improvements to actual visits
- Compare traffic before and after major changes (new content, agents, or technical fixes)
- See which AI models are driving the most referrals to your site
