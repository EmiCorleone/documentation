## What is it?

Instead of digging through charts and numbers, the Natural Language Report gives your team a written overview highlighting shifts in visibility, technical structure, competitor positioning, and citation patterns.

---

## What's included

Each report covers:

1. **Overall visibility score interpretation and trend**
2. **Position improvements and declines across prompts**
3. **Sentiment breakdown** — positive, neutral, negative
4. **Top-performing and underperforming prompts**
5. **Citation gap analysis**
6. **Competitor comparison narrative**
7. **Actionable recommendations**

---

## How to use it

Open the report from your dashboard. Filter by:

- **Time range:** 7 days, 14 days, 30 days, or custom
- **AI model:** ChatGPT, Claude, Perplexity, Gemini, or All

The report regenerates based on your selected filters, giving you a focused narrative for any slice of your data. You can download it as PDF or Markdown.

The Natural Language Report keeps AI search from becoming a black box by turning raw measurements into a narrative your whole team can act on.
